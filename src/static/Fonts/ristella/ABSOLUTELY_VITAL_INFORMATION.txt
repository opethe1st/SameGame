Ristella - a logotype script font. 
This high-quality typeface is created by Måns Grebäck. 
Its style is bold and has large, attention-grabbing uppercase letters. 
Ristella contains alternate and stylistic alternates, as well as multiple ligatures.
With its hundreds of glyphs it also has support for a wide range of languages. 

How do you make a swash?
Combine an ending and a tail to make a swash. 
Endings are found in the following symbols: [ ] { }
Tails are found in the following symbols:  < > § _ ¤ 
Example: Ristella}>

With ligatures activated, write multiple tail symbols to make a longer swash. 
Example: Tailextender]¤¤¤
